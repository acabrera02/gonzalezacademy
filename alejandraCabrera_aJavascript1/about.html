About Us

Welcome to Gonzalez Academy where we provide computer classes for low income families. Gonzalez Academy wants you to start your career growth by providing you with a variaty of classes.

Gonzalez Academy web site launched in September 2022 and our main focus is to help low income members to advanced in their career. Gonzalez Academy knows how important technology is for almost every role. If you want to transition to a different career or if you are simply courious and would like to improve your computer skills, join Gonzalez Academy for free today.
